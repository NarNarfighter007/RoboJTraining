Integer values
	byte is 8 bit
	short is 16-bit
	int is 32-bit (smaller range, smaller memory)
	long is 64-bit (bigger range, more memory)
	
Floating values
	float 32-bit decimal
	double 64-bit decimal (double the precision of the float)

Constants
	final does not mean constant
	final is IMMUTABLE (cannot be changed)
	
Enclose singular characters in '', NOT ""
